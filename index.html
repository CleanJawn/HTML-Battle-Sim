Welcome to the Arena!

You and an opponent will choose a warrior class and will duel!

A Knight uses their knowledge of the blade to fight valiently and their armor can be the difference between an enemies attack being a deathblow!

A Wizard uses their knowledge of the arcane arts to protect itself with dangerously powerful spells, but if an opponent is able
to get close enough to attack, it could prove dangerous for that wizard!

A Monk has mastered the arts of hand to hand combat and while not as deadly as a blade, their fists can inflict some serious damage.
Their nimble movements often can save their lives, and sometimes dodge blows entirely.

How to Fight:

1. Attack!
Damage can be affected by a targets innate defense.

2. Defend!
Damage taken will be cut in half if you are attacked next turn.

3. Wind Up!
Take a turn to windup so your next attack will do double damage.
